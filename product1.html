The unique teacup features Hokusai's Red Fuji, capturing the moment of the rising sun turning Mount Fuji red in a clear early morning.

With the combination of Kutani ware with the masterpiece of the genius artist Hokusai Katsushika, the teacup has truly an eye-stopping charm for anyone.

The natural shape, handcrafted by the potter's wheel, fits your hand comfortably and gives a relaxing atmosphere.

Red Fuji is known to bring good fortune, so It is highly recommended as a gift for someone who loves Japanese art and culture.
